Rotate YouTube Video

Enhance your YouTube experience by rotating videos to your preferred angle!

Rotate

  • support 0째, 90째, 180째, 270째
  • affect youtube video preview
  • * If HDR video has problems when rotated and colors wash out, it's a browser problem. You can turn off HDR

Zoom / Mirror

  • support zoom, mirror, move
  • support long press +,- button on Zoom/Mirror Menu
  • * the value will be reset after pressing the reset button or the rotate button
  • * not affect youtube video preview
  • * need to adjust value for normal mode and full screen mode

Shortcuts

  • Show Options Page : ctrl+shift+1
  • Rotate : ctrl+shift+9
  • Zoom in : ctrl+shift+.
  • Zoom out : ctrl+shift+,
  • Move left : ctrl+shift+arrow-left
  • Move right : ctrl+shift+arrow-right
  • Move up : ctrl+shift+arrow-up
  • Move down : ctrl+shift+arrow-down
  • Mirror : ctrl+shift+8
  • Reset Zoom/Move/Mirror Options : ctrl+shift+7
  • * Chrome has only 3 shortcuts: Show Options Page, Rotate, Mirror

Shortcuts with mouse

  • Zoom : on player > shift+scroll
  • Move : on player > shift+left-click+move
  • Reset Zoom/Move/Mirror : on player > shift+middle-click
